Our Services

Comprehensive Surfacing & Paving Solutions

We offer a full range of professional tar surfacing, asphalt paving, block paving, and sports court construction services for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. Our experienced team ensures every project is completed to the highest standards of quality and durability.

Tar Surfacing

  • Driveways & Parking Lots
  • Pothole Patching & Crack Sealing
  • Asphalt Resurfacing (Overlay)
  • Surface Dressing & Chip Sealing
  • Tar Rejuvenation & Seal Coating
  • Speed Humps & Road Markings

Brick Paving & Block Paving

  • Brick and block paving installation
  • Driveways, pathways, and patios
  • Interlocking and cobblestone patterns
  • Edge trimming and drainage solutions
  • Paving maintenance and repairs

Tennis & Sports Courts

  • Tennis court construction and resurfacing
  • Netball, basketball, and multi-sport courts
  • Acrylic and asphalt court finishes
  • Line marking and coating maintenance
  • Drainage and surface leveling

Concrete Surfacing

  • Concrete driveways, walkways, and floors
  • Reinforced concrete slabs and foundations
  • Exposed aggregate and brushed finishes
  • Industrial and commercial concrete surfacing
  • Joint sealing and surface smoothing
